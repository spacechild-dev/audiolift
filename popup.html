Dynamic Range Compression Guide

What is compression?

Compression reduces the difference between quiet and loud sounds, making dialogue easier to hear while preventing loud scenes from being too loud.

Threshold

The volume level where compression starts working.

  • -24dB: Moderate (default)
  • -35dB: Aggressive (dialogue preset)
  • Lower = more compression

Ratio

How much to compress sounds above the threshold.

  • 3:1: Gentle (default)
  • 6:1: Strong (dialogue preset)
  • Higher = more aggressive

Knee

How smoothly compression engages.

  • 30dB: Smooth, natural (default)
  • 15dB: Aggressive, noticeable
  • Higher = more natural

Quick Tips

For quiet dialogue: Threshold -35dB, Ratio 6:1

For movies: Threshold -30dB, Ratio 4:1

For music: Threshold -24dB, Ratio 3:1
