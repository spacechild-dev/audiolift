Dynamic Compression

What is compression?

Compression reduces the difference between quiet and loud sounds, making dialogue easier to hear.

Threshold

Volume level where compression starts.

  • -24dB: Moderate (default)
  • -35dB: Aggressive
  • Lower = more compression

Ratio

How much to compress.

  • 3:1: Gentle (default)
  • 6:1: Strong
  • Higher = more aggressive

Knee

Smoothness of compression.

  • 30dB: Smooth (default)
  • 15dB: Aggressive
  • Higher = more natural
